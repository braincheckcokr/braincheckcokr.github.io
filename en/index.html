The Essence of Language Learning
Handled with Precision

BrainCheck is a language learning community platform that uses flashcards as its core abstraction to precisely address the essence of language learning.

What We Do

Language learning may seem complex, but it can essentially be summarized into two processes.

  • Listening is the process of recognizing sounds and determining correct answers
  • Speaking is the process of converting thoughts into language and verifying their accuracy

BrainCheck abstracts all these processes into a simple flashcard interface.

Behind the scenes, it continuously tracks the learner's comprehension, retention rate, and forgetting speed.

It adjusts the learning flow so that learners always remain in the 'optimal memory state'.

Technology & Experience

BrainCheck is built around these core technologies to simultaneously elevate both learning efficiency and experience.

ML-Based Recommendation System

Analyzes learner comprehension and forgetting patterns to select the next study material at the optimal timing

High-Quality Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Our Principles

1. Perfection comes from removal

"Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

BrainCheck keeps removing to focus on the essence rather than piling on features.

2. Learning is a process of 'arrival'

Learning is not accumulating information, but reaching a state where you can arrive at the truth of a subject on your own.

We design so that users reach understanding through intuition and deduction.

3. Easy explanations are short and show the whole picture

An easy explanation is not a detailed one. It's one that makes the other person feel they "understand" in a short time.

This comes not from individual pieces of information, but from showing the structure and perspective of the entire situation.

Every learning experience in BrainCheck is designed based on this principle.
