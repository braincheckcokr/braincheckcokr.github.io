Brain Check Refund Policy

This page explains the standards for Brain Check Plus subscription payments and refunds. It covers subscription cancellation, refund requests, and how AI Credits and storage are handled.

Effective date and last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your Brain Check Plus subscription through the in-app subscription management flow before the next billing date. Cancellation stops automatic renewal from the next billing date.

Cancellation generally does not mean an automatic refund for the current paid subscription period. If a refund is not approved, you can continue using subscription benefits until the end of the current billing period.

2. How to Request a Refund

You can request a refund through the platform where you made the purchase (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

  • Apple(App Store) refund request: Request an Apple refund
  • Google Play refund request: Request a Google Play refund

3. Refund Review Criteria

Refund policies follow the policies of each store.

4. AI Credits and Storage

  • Plus includes 50GB storage and 20,000 AI Credits per month.
  • AI Credits are an internal service usage unit and have no cash value.
  • Unused AI Credits expire monthly and do not roll over to the next month.
  • Unused, expired, or deducted AI Credits are not eligible for separate cash refunds or cash-equivalent compensation.
  • If a refund is approved, subscription benefits, remaining AI Credits, and subscription storage access may be restricted immediately or within a reasonable period.

5. Yearly Subscription Refunds

Yearly subscriptions are offered at a discounted price for annual use. Plus yearly is US$43. If a refund is approved, the refund amount and processing method may depend on applicable law, usage period, and service usage status.

6. Processing Time

If a refund is approved, the refund is processed to the original payment method. The timing of the actual deposit or reversal may vary by card issuer, bank, payment method, country, and processing procedure.

This refund policy does not limit your statutory rights. If applicable consumer protection laws or the conditions displayed during checkout are more favorable to you than this policy, those conditions may apply first.
